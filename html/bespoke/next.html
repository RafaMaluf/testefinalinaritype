Tég © Creative Intelligence Bureau presents a fresh integrated identity for the digital bank "next", based on the company's new motto "You Happen" (Você Acontece, in Portuguese), a discourse that brings customers realizations as its core message, now standing as the center of its communication efforts.
The typeface glyphs are defined by an abstraction of the Elastic's geometry, combined with the humanist sans-serif typeface. That's noticeable by the symbol's particular sharp counterforms and rounded corners. In one side, the functionality of a sober paragraph, on the other, the magnetism of attractive titles. As a result, this vast amount of resources allows the typeface to move through in different contexts and express many different typographic voices.
