Inari Type is an independent type foundry with Japanese roots, specializing in the design of both Latin and non-Latin typefaces. Founded in 2020 by Caio Kondo and based in São Paulo, Brazil, we craft retail fonts and custom typefaces for a diverse range of organizations, from large corporations to small businesses. For more information or inquiries, please feel free to contact us.
Services:
Typeface design
Logotype creation
Logotype refinement
